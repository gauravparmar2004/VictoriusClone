Why Work With an Ecommerce SEO Company?

Data from the Ecommerce Foundation’s United States Ecommerce Country Report indicates that 88% of customers start their buying journey with search engines.

These buyers want to make an informed decision and are looking for reviews, comparisons, and other information that can help them purchase from online stores with confidence.

While paid search and display ads will bring some traffic to your website, ad blockers and ad blindness reduce the ROI of your advertising spend. And the minute you stop paying for ads, they stop driving traffic.

On the other hand, while a search engine optimization campaign requires an upfront investment, its effectiveness compounds over time and brings in better converting customers.

If your ecommerce website doesn’t appear in search engine results when buyers search for product information, you’ll lose customers to other ecommerce websites that have invested in optimizing their category pages for search engines.

Drive Organic Ecommerce Sales with a Measurable ROI

Google Analytics Ecommerce Tracking

For ecommerce businesses whose point of sale sits directly on their website, the Google Analytics (GA) ecommerce tracking feature provides a comprehensive overview of data like average order value, number of orders, and billing location.

With GA ecommerce tracking set up correctly, we can correlate sales data with your organic traffic channel.

From there, determining the ROI of our ecommerce SEO services becomes a simple calculation of dollars invested and increased revenue attributed to organic traffic.

Our customized reporting dashboard provides clear insights that are easily translatable to stakeholders in your organization.



Google Analytics Goal Tracking

While revenue generation is a compelling metric to track, other types of conversions might serve as leading indicators for future sales.

Our ecommerce SEO agency can set up Google Analytics to track important events on your website, such as email sign-ups, wishlist additions, or social media shares.

By monitoring metrics upstream of sales, you can connect organic traffic increases to top-of-funnel opportunities to nurture new customers.

This kind of tracking demonstrates the value of SEO to future-proof your revenue generation.
